About Me

Eng: Abdullah Wasel was born on 12-07-2001, in Khamer District, Amran Governorate, Yemen. He began his education at Al-Shaheed Mohammed Al-Durra School in Al-Mahafeeth village, then moved to Sana'a where he completed his high school education. Abdullah advanced his English studies at New Horizons Institute and pursued multiple courses in human development, business management, and e-commerce at Yemen Soul Institute, earning several professional certificates. With strong expertise in Photoshop design, Frontend programming, and networking, he has also built solid skills in modem programming, system administration, and cloud computing. Passionate about cybersecurity, he studied Wi-Fi network penetration online and is now teaching others Wi-Fi hacking techniques at Cyberman Academy. Currently, Abdullah is in his fourth year of studying Information Technology at Saba University, where he is recognized among the top students. He is driven by a passion for technology, innovation, and continuous learning.
